    Quizapp open_in_new

    This quiz app is an interactive multiple-choice question system that displays one question at a time, updates navigation buttons dynamically, and tracks the user's selected answers. Each question shows its difficulty level, topic, and marks. Users can move between questions with “Next” and “Previous” buttons, and when they submit, the app automatically grades all answers, highlights correct and incorrect options, and displays a final score with pass/fail status. The app also includes a restart feature, allowing users to retake the quiz after confirmation. An explanation section can be expanded to show the reasoning for each answer after submission
